NEWLY BUILT HOME WITH EVERYTHING IN REACH!

This 4 Bedroom, 2 Bathroom Air Conditioned residence is IDEAL for Families wanting a spacious home with the convenience of everything in reach, or Professional Executives looking for a fresh stylish residence with all the modern luxuries!!

Peacefully located in a nice quiet pocket of Kardinya that’s just steps to picturesque cycling / walking paths, and just minutes to Murdoch University as well as St John of God and Fiona Stanley Hospitals, and even all the convenience of Kardinya Park Shopping Centre!

- Other Bedrooms with mirrored Built In Robes

- Smart Wired
- Dual Driveway (room for a boat!)
- Alfresco entertaining area
